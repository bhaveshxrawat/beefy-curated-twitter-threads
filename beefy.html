I was bookmarking Twitter threads which I found worthy, and I saw other were doing it too, some were saving it in their Notion account, and few others were doing it their way. While there's nothing wrong with any of these methods, but, finding, managing those threads later became cumbersome for me. Bookmarks doesn't have any kind of filtering feature.

One might not want to log-in to its Notion account in a foreign device just to find/refer that specific thread.

So I decided to take this issue as an opportunity to make a huge, beefy, curated list of valuable Twitter threads. But that's not even the best part... the best part is that you'll be able to contribute your favourite threads that you think brings some sort of learning/value to the table.

If you're interested in contributing, and would like to be a part of this good cause, there will be a Google Form where you'll be able to slap in the thread's link, and after the successful review your contribution may be added.
